About

It's nice to meet you.

Andrea Nicholson is a full-stack web developer, artist, and all-around nerd based in Raleigh, NC. After graduating from East Carolina University in 2013 with a BFA in Animation and Interactive Design, Andrea returned to the triangle and began working for Wake County Public Libraries as a Youth Services Library Assistant, using her Google-fu and passion for information accessibility for the benefit of her local community. When not working, you can find Andrea painting, reading or video gaming at home despite the best distracting efforts of her two mischievous cats.
